General Rules:


Event-1 Rules: ULTIMO INGENIERO





PHASE 01- “PUZZLE TECH"


Each team will be given a set of jigsaw puzzle.
A picture will be displayed on the screen for a period of 30sec, participants have to observe the picture carefully and assemble the puzzle within the timeframe given.
This is the screening stage, teams arranging the maximum pieces of jigsaw within short time will be qualified for the next phase.



PHASE 02 – “30MIN TECH CHALLENGE” & “TECHNICAL TREASURE HUNT”


30min tech challenge Technical treasure hunt

Different set of technical as well as general knowledge question will be displayed on screen the participants have to answer them all within certain time.
Based on Time and accuracy of answers, points will be allotted.
A common question will be asked to all the teams, participants must answer the question and obtain JCE map, which will be helpful in hunt.
Each different teams will be given different puzzle at first step, leading to different targets, as the game progresses the target goes on combining. Teams reaching the goal first will be allotted higher points then the other.

NOTE:-BY ADDING THE POINTS FROM BOTH THE EVENTS FINAL TEAMS WILL BE SELECTED TO PLAY IN THE FINAL PHASE.



PHASE 03- “BID AND BUILD”


->EACH TEAM WILL BE GIVEN A SPEICIFIED AMOUNT OF POINTS.
->Different resources will be displayed in the room for 30 minutes teams can examine the items within the time, and submit a model title to co-ordinators. After the submission of titles AUCTION will be conducted for every item, teams must bid for the materials they require for their specific model within the allotted points.
->After the auction is completed teams can start preparing models .based upon percentage of completion of model the WINNER and RUNNERS UP will be chosen
