General Rules:




Event-1 Rules: Robo Soccer



 The matches will be conducted in knockout manner.
 Lords will be conducted before the start of the event.
 The robots will be verified before the start of the event.
 Registration closes at 11.00 am on 6/04/2018.
 Judges decision will be final.
 Each half is 5 minutes.
 Venue: C-building Entrance; Time: 11am onwards.

1. A team can consist of a maximum of 2 members.
2. A team is allowed to play with same robot only.
3. Knowingly If any kind of damage done to the arena will be disqualified.
4. All the required accessories have to be brought by the participants.
5. No technical assistance will be provided by the coordinators during the time of the event.
6. No practice runs will be provided.
7. Use of an IC engine in any form is not allowed.
8. Human interference (e.g. touching the robot, stepping into the arena) during the game is not allowed.
9. If a participant does not comply with the rules of the event he/she will be disqualified from the event.
10. A robot with the base of a toy car and its gearbox as a machine part will be disqualified. Also, LEGO kits are strictly prohibited and will lead to disqualification.
11. Participants with wired robots are strictly advised to get wires of length 3m or more.
12. In case of wired robots the wired should be slacked through out the game.
13. Readymade PCB’s are not allowed.



ROBOT SPECIFICATIONS


1. The Maximum dimensions of the robot is 40cm x 30cm x 30cm(l*b*h).
2. Robots can be wired, wireless, or autonomous. In case of wired robots the wire should remain slack throughout the round.
3. Power supply to the robots should not exceed 12V.
4. Power supply can be ON/OFF board the bot.
5. The robots must use DC motors only.
6. Tolerance of 5% on dimensions and power supply will be allowed.
7. The robot's weight should not exceed 5kg.
8. For wireless robots make sure to use a dual frequency Transmitter to avoid interference.
